## BOSS: EBENEZER SCROOGE

**AC:** 16 | **HP:** 75 | **Speed:** 30 ft (can teleport 30 ft in coal dust)  
**Proficiency:** +3 | **Str:** 10 | **Dex:** 14 (+2) | **Con:** 14 (+2) | **Int:** 16 | **Wis:** 14 | **Cha:** 18

### WEAPONS:
- **Money-Counting Staff:** +6 to hit, 2d6+4 bludgeoning + 1d6 cold
- **Coal Bomb (Legendary Action):** 3d6 fire, 10 ft radius, DC 14 Dex save

### SPECIAL ABILITIES:
- **Damage Dampening Chains (Reaction, 2/round):** Reduce incoming damage by 10
- **Legendary Actions (2 points/round):**
  - **Move (1):** Teleport 30 ft in coal dust
  - **Bitter Monologue (1):** Frighten one target within 30 ft (DC 15 Wis save)
  - **Cast/Attack (2):** Command spell or coal bomb
- **Legendary Resistance (1/day):** Auto-succeed on failed save
- **Counterspell (1/day)**

### LOCATION BY TIME:
- **8:00-9:00 PM:** Grand Hall (with hostages)
- **9:00-10:00 PM:** Moving through workshop (checking vault operation)
- **10:00 PM-Midnight:** Clock Tower (final confrontation, sabotaging countdown)

**SPECIAL:** Wears The Naughty & Nice List on his belt (DC 16 Sleight of Hand to steal)

## TACTICAL NOTES

### GREMLINS:
- Flee at 3 HP or less
- Distracted by shiny objects (DC 13 Deception/Persuasion)
- Work in packs (advantage on attacks)
- Will steal equipment if given chance

### SCROOGE:
- Opens with Hold Person on "kindest" PC
- Teleports every round to avoid melee
- Retreats to Clock Tower at 25 HP
- Can be REDEEMED (DC 18 Persuasion, advantage with clues from Coal Processing)

### FROSTBITE:
- Commands other enemies
- Saves Counterspell for critical spells
- Protects hostages/vault as living shield

### GENERAL ENEMY BEHAVIOR:
- Most enemies surrender or flee at half HP
- Gremlins always flee at 3 HP
- Radio check-ins with Scrooge every 30 minutes
- Will call for backup if players are too loud

## TIME-BASED ENEMY POSITIONS

### 8:00 PM (Game Start)
- **Grand Hall:** Scrooge + Frostbite + 2-3 Gingerbread Guards (HOSTAGES)
- **Loading Dock:** Gumball, Licorice + 3 gremlins
- **Assembly Floor:** Gingersnap + 2 gremlins
- **Sleigh Hangar:** Charlie, Patty + 2 gremlins (wiring bomb)

- **Testing Chamber:** Tinsel, Frost Twins + 2 gremlins
- **Furnace:** Ember & Ash
