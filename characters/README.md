## Party Composition Notes

**Balanced Party:** All 6 characters work well together!
- **Tanks:** Ginger (Paladin), Northy (Ranger)
- **Damage:** Jolly (Artificer), Peppermint (Wizard), Holly (Rogue), Northy (Ranger)
- **Healing:** Ginger (Lay on Hands), Cinnamon (Druid), Jolly (Cure Wounds)
- **Support:** Ginger (Aura), Cinnamon (Buffs/Debuffs), Jolly (Infusions)
- **Face:** Ginger (Persuasion), Holly (Deception)
- **Skills:** Holly (Stealth/Acrobatics), Jolly (Arcana/Investigation), Northy (Animal Handling/Survival)

**Smaller Groups (3-4 players):**
- **Core Trio:** Ginger + Peppermint + Northy (Tank, Blaster, Damage)
- **Sneaky Squad:** Holly + Cinnamon + Jolly (Stealth, Support, Tech)
- **Balanced Four:** Ginger + Holly + Peppermint + Northy

## Quick Start Tips

1. **Read "Why Not at Party"** - Each character has a reason they weren't captured
2. **Know Your Role** - Combat role and special abilities
3. **Improvise!** - You start without your real gear - be creative!
4. **Use Specialist Knowledge** - Each character knows certain areas intimately
5. **Work Together** - This is a team heist, not solo heroics
